15 years ago we created and published an online game, where users could play one platformer level each day of December from 1st - 24th. Like in a classic advent calendar a new level opens behind each day/door.

Trouble in Video Game Land! Villain El Pix has stolen important files and taken them to Snowdriftland. Chubby Snow, who up to now has played rather unfortunate roles in games, is the only Pixel Hero suited for a rescue mission in the ice-cold realm.

Run, jump and slide through 4 different worlds, with 6 levels each, making 24 levels in total! Then take on El Pix in a final battle!

On Chubby’s adventure you will encounter many wacky bustling creatures trying to thwart your mission. These are only a few of them...
